Learn Anything at Slate in Abids

Slate – The School is recognised as one of the best schools in Abids, Hyderabad. With many years of excellence, Slate has pioneered a fresh, student-centred approach to education in the city.

As the first Slate campus here, we champion a learning philosophy free from commercial pressures, focused solely on making a meaningful impact on every student’s life. Today, with eight thriving campuses, Slate – The School continues to uphold this legacy.

Our curriculum and teaching embody high quality, relevance, forward-thinking values, and strong ethics. We complement the Cambridge curriculum with our distinctive, expert-designed SMAART and Sampoornatha Programs, thoughtfully crafted to equip students for a technology-driven future.

Our campus continues to be regarded as one of the best schools in Abids, Hyderabad, and is trusted by both parents and experts.
