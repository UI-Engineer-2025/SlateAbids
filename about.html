About our school

Founded in 2001 by the Vasireddy Educational Society, our institution has steadily grown to become one of the best schools in Hyderabad, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh. Through SLATE- The School, Mr. Vasireddy Amarnath wanted to impart quality and value-based education to children, improve the ethical standards in the field of education, and adopt a futuristic approach to promote traditional values amongst the younger generation.
